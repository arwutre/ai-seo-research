## Post Theme 1: "AEO vs. SEO — Two Parallel Discovery Systems"

Bernard's core thesis is that search has diverged into two parallel systems: traditional keyword-based ranking and AI-driven conversational discovery. Brands must optimize for both.

**Key takeaway:** AEO is a new layer on top of SEO — not a replacement. Both must be addressed.

---

## Post Theme 2: "The Long Long Tail — AI Validation Queries"

AI models perform thousands of granular web searches to validate information. Bernard advises targeting these "validation queries" rather than just high-volume user keywords.

**Key takeaway:** Map the validation queries AI models use behind the scenes, and create content that specifically addresses them.

---

## Post Theme 3: "Entity-Rich Content for AI Citation"

Content must contain the specific entities, features, and contextual signals that AI models expect. Clearscope analyzes what entities appear in AI responses to inform content creation.

**Key takeaway:** Treat entities, not keywords, as the fundamental building blocks of content strategy.

---

## Post Theme 4: "From Traffic to Brand Imprinting"

As AI surfaces answers directly, traffic volume declines. Bernard suggests KPIs must shift to "brand imprinting," "share of model," and "brand mentions."

**Key takeaway:** Track AI citations and brand mentions as primary KPIs, not just organic traffic.
