## Post Theme 3: "When Programmatic SEO Fails — The Patterns"

**Date Range:** Late 2025
**Source:** LinkedIn / X

Jake analyzed common failure patterns in programmatic SEO, noting that most failures come from: (1) targeting queries with no real user intent, (2) creating thin content without unique data or value, and (3) ignoring technical SEO fundamentals like internal linking and crawl budget.

> "Programmatic SEO fails when people treat it like a content farm. It works when you treat it like a product — each page needs to deliver genuine value."

**Key takeaway:** Successful programmatic SEO requires product-level thinking about each page's value proposition, not just template-based mass production.

## Post Theme 4: "B2B SaaS and AI Content — The Low-Hanging Fruit"

**Date Range:** Q1 2026
**Source:** LinkedIn

Jake discussed specific B2B SaaS content opportunities that AI excels at: comparison pages, integration pages, feature pages targeting long-tail queries, and glossary/educational content. These are high-value, formulaic enough for AI to handle well, and often ignored by competitors.

> "The best AI content opportunities in B2B SaaS aren't blog posts. They're the 500 comparison pages, 200 integration pages, and 100 feature pages your competitors haven't built yet."

**Key takeaway:** In B2B SaaS, AI-powered programmatic content delivers highest ROI on structured, formulaic page types that competitors neglect.
